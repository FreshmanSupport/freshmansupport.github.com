Community Managers

Be the first impression of your university to incoming freshmen. Engage social media and build your resume as a Community Manager with Freshman Support.

What is a community manager?

Community Managers provide an unbiased voice for your university, offering tips and helpful insight about student life, the good and the bad. Rising freshmen need help planning for college, and your involvement can help them tremendously. Share insight on housing, roommates, classes, campus life, student organizations financial aid or any other aspect of campus life you enjoy.

What will you gain as a community manager?

  • Resume builder
  • Automatic enrollment in a non-essay based, monthly scholarship pool
  • Opportunity to share your school pride
  • Social media campaign management experience
  • Published blogs
  • Personalized profile showcasing of your campus involvement
  • "Day in the Life" video feature of your college life
  • College planning packet to showcase your profile to rising freshmen
  • Access to optional weekly business seminars
  • Optional internship credit

What are we looking for?

  • A commitment of only 5 hours a week
  • School spirit and desire to help rising freshmen
  • Ability to meet basic goals and simple deadlines
  • Patience and respectfulness (freshmen may have some funny questions)

For more information on Community Managers click here for a PDF download or visit The Real Life.

Internships

Join a fun startup in Austin, TX. Learn about business, get mentorship and build your resume as an Intern with Freshman Support.

Who can apply for an internship?

Freshman Support accepts all majors and offers a variety of positions.

What will you gain as an intern?

  • Resume builder
  • Professional references and advocated for your career
  • Personalized profile showcasing of your work and campus life
  • Access to optional weekly business seminars
  • Access to our yearly social media conference
  • Direct mentorship from company executives
  • Social media training
  • Free trip to New Orleans sponsored by the company

What are we looking for?

  • A commitment of only 6 hours a week
  • School spirit and desire to help rising freshmen
  • Ability to meet basic goals and simple deadlines
  • Patience and respectfulness

For more information on internships click here for a PDF download.
