Admissions Q&A

  • Question:

    How important are admissions essays in getting accepted to UT?

    Answer:

    Admissions essay are used to determine many different things: acceptance into The University of Texas, and acceptance to the specific College in which you are applying to. Make sure all submitted essays are clear and concise and fit the assigned topics. Avoid any grammatical mistakes. The deadlines for UT's application for incoming freshman is May1st. Make sure to have your essays proofread, edited, and submitted before this date.
