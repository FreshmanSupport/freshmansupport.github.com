The "Low Down" on Becoming a Community Manager

Why be a Community Manager?Build your resume, and be the mentor you wish you had as an entering freshman. Share your school pride and answer questions directly from incoming students on FreshmanSupport.com. On the site, you'll have your own profile where we'll recognize your campus involvement and publish several features on YOU! You'll also be automatically enrolled in a non-essay based scholarship as well!
What is a Commmunity Manager?Community Managers are the first impression of rising freshmen considering your university! Community Managers share student experiences through social media posts and webinars, along with a profile and a "Day in the Life" video feature. Community Managers answer questions directly from incoming freshmen on FreshmanSupport.com.
What's in it for you?
  • Strong Resume Builder
  • Scholarships Awarded to Best Community Managers
  • Personalized Profile Showcasing Your Student Life
  • Access to Biweekly Business Seminars
  • Professional References
What's the time commitment?5 Hours (or less) a Week
