Top 10 Things To Do in Austin

Experiencing your city's attractions will help you make a better decision on which school to pick. So when you're done touring and learning about your school campus and housing areas, you should take some time to enjoy some fun things in the city. Based on student recommendations, we have compiled a list of the top 10 fun things to do during your visit!

  • 1

    Dirty 6th

    Texas' best known street. A seven block stretch between Congress and IH 35 where celebrities and people like you go to get crunk. The location draws an electric bunch including endless streams of mostly students, some interesting street folk, and lots of out of town visitors.
